# Ink's Journal

## Visual Constraints
*   **Style:** Modern graphic novel aesthetic.
    *   **Lines:** Bold black outlines (`stroke="black" stroke-width="2"` or thicker).
    *   **Colours:** Vibrant flat colours.
    *   **Effects:** Halftone patterns (`url(#sky-halftone)`), cel-shading, and comic-style "glow" filters.
