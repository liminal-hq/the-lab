# Ink's Visual Journal

## Visual Motifs

### Comic Book Style
- **Outlines:** Thick black strokes (`stroke-width="2"` or `3`) are essential for characters and key objects.
- **Colours:** Use flat, vibrant colours. Avoid gradients unless necessary for specific effects (like "glow").
- **Textures:** Halftone patterns (`sky-halftone`) add depth and retro feel.
- **Typography:** Impact or Comic Sans fonts fit the playful, chaotic tone.
- **Animations:** Simple bobbing or wiggling animations bring the scene to life.

### Collectibles
- **Pizza:** Represents the ultimate prize. Rendered with cheese (`#f1c40f`), crust (`#e67e22`), and pepperoni (`#c0392b`).
- **Glow:** Key items should have a subtle glow or "scent lines" to indicate importance.
